About Us

At Blockbuster, we celebrate the legacy of a golden age of cinema and entertainment. Our goal is to keep the nostalgia of Those days when you go to the video store and pick a movie It was quite an experience. Today, we bring that same excitement to the digital world. Although times have changed, our passion for movies remain the same. Blockbuster is more than just a website, It is a tribute to cinephiles at heart. Here you can discover Classic films, recent hits and selected recommendations by our team, so that you always find something that inspires you. Our mission is to offer you a unique experience, where every film that you choose transports you to new adventures, unforgettable stories and moments of entertainment at home, just like you did when you you rented your favorite tape in the video store.
